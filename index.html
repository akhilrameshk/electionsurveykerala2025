Election Survey Kerala 2025

This independent survey collects feedback from voters in local body wards across Kerala. Please participate using the form below. It takes less than five minutes to complete.

Participate in the survey

Please scroll inside the form, answer all relevant questions, and click Submit at the end.

About this survey

The questionnaire includes simple multiple-choice questions about party preference, performance of the current local body government, and the likely winner in your ward. Your responses help build ward‑wise analysis of party support and key local issues.

Results will be published as charts and tables for each ward, showing overall trends such as which party is leading, how satisfied people are with local services, and how many voters are undecided. These insights are for civic awareness and research only.

Privacy and consent

This form may collect your email address only to prevent duplicate responses. Email addresses will not be shared or used for campaigning. You may skip any question you are not comfortable answering. By submitting the form, you agree that your responses can be used in aggregated, anonymised reports.

This site is for educational and civic research purposes.
